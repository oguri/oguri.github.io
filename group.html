Group

Cosmology group
  • Our research group at Center for Frontier Science, Chiba University was formed in 2022 and covers a wide range of topics in theoretical and observational cosmology. We are also a part of Astrophysics Laboratory at Department of Physics and closely work with other astrophysics research groups.
For prospective students
  • See this page for opportunities of graduate school admissions. Unfortunately, in Japan, it is not common that students receive full financial support from their supervisors or University, so I would recommend applying for any scholarship (such as MEXT scholarship) if you are interested in applying for our graduate program.
For prospective postdocs
  • Please contact me if you are interested in choosing me as a host researcher of JSPS postdoctoral fellowship.
